About Rebuilding Nepal

Our mission to empower communities and support the youth movement for a better Nepal.

Our Story

Rebuilding Nepal was founded in response to the growing need for community support and youth empowerment across Nepal. We believe in the power of collective action and the importance of supporting Gen-Z protests that advocate for positive change.

Our Mission

To rebuild and strengthen communities through volunteer efforts, resource distribution, and supporting youth-led initiatives that drive social and political change.

Our Vision

A Nepal where every community thrives, youth voices are heard, and positive change is driven by collective action and mutual support.

Our Values

Community First

We prioritize the needs of local communities and work directly with residents to ensure our efforts make a meaningful impact.

Youth Empowerment

We support and amplify the voices of young Nepalis who are driving positive change through peaceful protests and advocacy.

Sustainability

Our projects are designed to create lasting change and build resilient communities that can thrive long-term.

Transparency

We maintain open communication about our work, finances, and impact to build trust with our supporters and communities.
